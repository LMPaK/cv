Lisa King

RN, MSN

I am a wife and mother of two that has made the decision to change to a different career field. I have a background in pediatric nursing and training in Women's Health Nursing. I enjoy palm trees and warm weather, spending time with my family and two dogs, and baking recipes passed down from my late father and late great aunt. I am most at home by the water, whether it be on a boat fishing, or on the beach relaxing to the sound of waves. When I have a little time to myself, I also enjoy genealogy and discovering how my ancestor's lived and settled in America.

After taking some time away from my career to grow our family and become a foster mother, COVID hit. Between the pandemic and a loved one dying, I did some soul-searching and decided to make a career change. I needed a career that was more stable, flexible, and safe for my family. My brother was working as a software developer at the time, and I found myself wanting a lot of what his career offered him.

Throughout college I had taken Web Development and Nursing Informatics courses. While working as a secretary for a construction company, I had even built a website for my employer. Remembering all the times that computers and the internet had been weaved into my personal and professional life, I realized it was still something I was passionate about and quite honestly more comfortable with than nursing. I built that website around the age of 19, and now nearly twenty years later I am ready to get back to where I first began. Back then it seemed like it was such a male-dominated field, so it is encouraging to now see so many women in tech. I know it will be challenging, but I am driven, self-motivated, and of course, excited!
